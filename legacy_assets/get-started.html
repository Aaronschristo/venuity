What Is It?

A Smart RFID Guest Pass

The Venuity Product Card is a passive RFID chip embedded in a durable PVC card (or silicone wristband). It stores a unique guest token that communicates with Venuity hardware modules placed at every zone entry point in your venue.

💳

RFID Card

Slim, durable PVC card — ₹15 per unit. Ideal for walk-in guests and day passes.

⌚

RFID Wristband

Soft silicone wristband — ₹20 per unit. Perfect for kids and active play zones.

🔒

Unique Token

Each card is provisioned with a cryptographically unique guest ID — no two are alike.

⚡

Instant Read

Sub-100ms tap response via our ESP32-powered reader modules, so queues never form.

How It Works

5 Steps from Purchase to Play

The entire guest journey is managed automatically — no manual ticketing, no cash handling, no disputes.

01

Purchase & Provision

At the reception kiosk, the operator selects a play package. The system writes the guest's session (duration, zones, balance) to the card's RFID token in real time via the Venuity dashboard.

🖥️
02

Card / Wristband Issued

The guest receives their physical card or wristband. No app download, no account creation — just tap and play.

💳
03

Tap to Enter Zones

Each zone entry has a Venuity RFID reader. The guest taps their card; the gate opens only if the session is valid and the zone is included in their package. The display shows remaining time.

🔓
04

Real-Time Cloud Sync

Every tap event is pushed instantly to the Venuity cloud. Operators monitor occupancy, session timers, and revenue across all zones from a single unified dashboard.

☁️
05

Session Ends & Card Reset

When the session expires, the gate locks automatically. Staff can top-up the card for another session or the card is returned to be reprogrammed — reducing wastage.
